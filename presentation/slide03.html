Transacting Over Time

  • Unlike banking apps, crypto wallets don’t offer payment dates in the future.
  • It’s not possible to put an unexecuted payment on a blockchain. A contract can do it but it needs the tokens.
  • To make a crypto payment it must be initiated.
  • A money stream could be configured with a variable rate.
  • It starts with a negligible 18 decimal amount. Dust.
  • Then on a specified date the rate goes up.
  • It streams until it’s done.
